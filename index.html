Operational Framework

RentStyle Income Ecosystem

RentStyle's financial model ensures high-quality fabric hygiene, community affordability, and strategic designer sponsorships.

1. Transactional Service Fees

A dynamic transactional commission is computed on bookings (15% for rentals, 10% for purchases) to maintain system platform hosting and showroom logistical operations.

2. Monthly Membership (StylePass)

Subscription packages like StylePass Gold build consistent recurring cash flows while rewarding users with waived commissions and 15% to 25% off catalog items.

3. Damage Protection Fees

Optional micro-protection additions starting from QAR 15 per outfit cover unexpected fabric accidents. It delivers peace of mind for clients and creates high-margin insurance revenue pools.

4. Sponsored Designer Collections

Elite fashion designers, wedding boutiques, and international brands pay advertising premiums to list their collections as "Sponsored" for top placement on user grids.
