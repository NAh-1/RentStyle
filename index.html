Operational Framework

RentStyle Income Ecosystem

RentStyle's financial model ensures high-quality fabric hygiene, community affordability, and strategic designer sponsorships.

1. Transactional Service Fees

A clean 5% transactional commission is computed on standard one-off rental bookings to maintain system platform hosting and showroom logistical operations.

2. Monthly Membership (StylePass)

Subscription packages like **StylePass Gold** (QAR 149/mo) and **Platinum** (QAR 299/mo) build consistent recurring cash flows while rewarding users with waived transactional commissions, deposit waivers, and 15% to 25% off catalog items.

3. Damage Protection Fees

Optional micro-protection additions starting from **QAR 15 per outfit** cover unexpected fabric accidents. It delivers peace of mind for clients and creates high-margin insurance revenue pools.

4. Sponsored Designer Collections

Elite fashion designers, wedding boutiques, and international couture brands pay advertising premiums to list their collections as **"Sponsored Designers"** for top placement on user grids, promoting premium sales.
